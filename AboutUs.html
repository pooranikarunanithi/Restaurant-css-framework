About Us

In search of a restaurant with Indian cuisine, but don't know where to go? You'll also feel relaxed in our air-conditioned interior, no matter the outside temperature.

A restaurant with WiFi

Our Indian cuisine offers an amazing range of delicious, freshly made dishes just for you. Alongside our delicious food we also offer a range of refreshing drinks to complete your meal. We've always got a delicious lunch waiting for you. Our wide-ranging buffet of tasty dishes and delicacies offers you variety with every bite and is wide-ranging for families to share a meal together. Surf the web using our free WiFi and stay connected with your friends.

Delivery service available

Our restaurant is wheelchair accessible. It is possible to book a table using our special online reservation service. Vouchers, VISA or Ticket Restaurant as well as cash payments are accepted here. If you'd like to enjoy our food in the comfort of your own home, you can simply go ahead and use our delivery service or pick up your order for takeaway.
